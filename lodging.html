Transportation Info

Arriving by air? Our small airport welcomes you, with plans underway to accommodate larger jets soon. Prefer cruising? A weekly cruise ship docks at Yellow Leaf Bay. In Taniti City, public buses run daily from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. For farther exploration, private buses, taxis, rental cars, and bikes (helmets provided) are available. Taniti City is flat and walkable, and Merriton Landing offers easy exploration on foot.
